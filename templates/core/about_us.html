About Sharebite

Fighting food waste, one meal at a time

Our Mission

Sharebite is a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. We connect people, restaurants, and organizations who have surplus food with those who need it.

Nearly one-third of all food produced worldwide is wasted annually, while millions struggle with food insecurity. Sharebite provides a solution by creating a seamless connection between food donors and receivers, tracking environmental impact, and promoting sustainable living.
